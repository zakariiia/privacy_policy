This Privacy Policy describes how the Science Unit Converter app developed by SphinxLabs handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect absolutely no personal information. The Science Unit Converter app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind.

  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics
  • No crash reports sent to any server

2. Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your favorite unit conversions
  • Your conversion history
  • Your app settings (theme, decimal precision, default category)

This data never leaves your device and is never shared with anyone.

3. Internet Access

The Science Unit Converter app is 100% offline. It does not require or use an internet connection. No data is ever sent to any server.

The app does not use any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs.

Our app is safe for all ages. We do not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
